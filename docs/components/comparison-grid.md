# ComparisonGrid

`ComparisonGrid` is the governed comparison surface for templates that teach through contrast or classification.

## Purpose
- Hold 2 to 4 concepts in view at the same time
- Keep comparison criteria visible while the learner applies the distinction
- Provide a print-safe table-like fallback

## Content shape
- `title`
- `intro`
- `columns`
- `rows`
- `apply_prompt`

## Behaviour
- Static only in this phase
- Horizontal overflow is allowed on smaller screens

## Used by
- `compare-and-apply`
- `distinction-grid`
