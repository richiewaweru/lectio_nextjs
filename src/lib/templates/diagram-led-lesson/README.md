# Diagram-Led Lesson

## Intent
Teach through a sequence of diagrams so the learner sees the change before the prose explains it.

## Best For
- Biology and chemistry stage sequences
- Earth science processes with visible transitions
- Learners who benefit from stepwise visual progression

## Not Ideal For
- Single static visuals
- Topics without a meaningful stage progression

## Learner Fit
- Visual
- General

## Subjects
- Biology
- Chemistry
- Earth science

## Lesson Flow
Hook -> Diagram Series -> Explain -> Process -> Practice -> What Next

## Required Components
- SectionHeader
- HookHero
- DiagramSeries
- ExplanationBlock
- PracticeStack
- WhatNextBridge

## Optional Components
- ProcessSteps
- GlossaryRail
- PitfallAlert

## Why This Template Exists
When change over stages is the lesson, a single image is not enough. This template gives the learner a visible progression to track.

## Responsive Notes
The diagram series stays above the prose and the glossary support remains inline instead of moving into a sidebar.

## Print Notes
All stages print in order and the process notes expand below them.
